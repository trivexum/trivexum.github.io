About Us

Welcome to TRIVEXUM, your cozy corner of the web where learning meets creativity. We’re all about building fun, functional, and beginner-friendly mini-projects that not only teach you the core web dev trio — HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — but also get you **excited to code**!

We believe in learning by doing. That's why each project in TRIVEXUM is a chance to tinker, explore, and get your hands dirty (digitally, ofc 😄). Whether it's a slick calculator, a weather app, or something totally wild — there's something for everyone!

Got a spicy idea? We’re all ears! Drop us a line at trivexum@gmail.com or swing by the Contact Page. Collaboration is the heart of dev culture 💡💬
