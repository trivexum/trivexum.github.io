TRIVEXUM Update: LexiLookup is LIVE + Mobile Sidebar Added!

Hey word lovers and tech fam! 📚✨

Big news from the Trivexum dev cave — LexiLookup is now officially LIVE! It’s your ultimate word companion packed with all the essential language tools, wrapped in a clean UI that actually makes you want to learn. 😎 And that’s not all — we’ve also added a responsive mobile sidebar to make LexiLookup feel super smooth on small screens. 🎉📱

What is LexiLookup? 🔤

It’s not your average dictionary. LexiLookup helps you explore and understand words like a boss:

  • 📖 Definitions
  • 🔊 Pronunciation (Audio included!)
  • 🔁 Synonyms & Antonyms
  • 💬 Clean Example Sentences
  • 📸 LexiSnap – Capture any meaning as an image

Sidebar for Small Screens 📱

Mobile users, you’re in for a treat! We’ve rolled out a brand-new collapsible sidebar that works like a charm on phones and tablets. Whether you're searching, snapping, or switching views, this sidebar has your back.

Here’s what it brings:

  • ✅ Slide-in design
  • ✅ Clean mobile navigation
  • ✅ Easy feature access
  • ✅ Feels like a real app experience

Built with the TRIVEXUM Theme 🖌️

Of course, it uses the same beautiful theme as the rest of Portico apps.

It’s clean, consistent, and super easy on the eyes — just how we like it 😌

What’s Next? 👀

We’re already cooking up more features for future updates:

  • 🗓️ Word of the Day
  • 💾 Bookmark & Favorite Words
  • 📡 Offline Word Cache
  • 🧠 AI-powered mnemonics

Final Thoughts 💬

LexiLookup started as a simple idea — but now it’s a fully-featured word tool for curious learners, devs, writers, and anyone who’s ever typed "define ____" into a search bar. Try it, explore it, and don’t forget to share it with your crew. 💙

🔗 LexiLookup is now LIVE — dive in!
